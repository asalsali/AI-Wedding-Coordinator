## What Success Looks Like

WedFlow is live and serving 10-12 paid couples by September 1, 2026. The product handles guest SMS questions with AI replies in the couple's voice, escalates sensitive messages with push notifications, and the couple can manage everything from a PWA on their phone. A waitlist has captured demand from a 4-month marketing campaign, and B2B2C channel partners (churches, officiants, pre-marital counsellors, wedding planners) have registered interest.
